Installation Instructions

Installation Tools Needed:

  • Pocket knife or scissors
  • Pliers
  • Power Drill
  • 1⁄4” Drill bit

Instructions:

  • Lower Attic Door.
  • Cut off existing cord.
  • Drill 1⁄4” hole where string was mounted (if required).
  • Insert the Fashion Pull-Ring through the 1⁄4” hole on the exterior side of the Attic Door. Hand-tighten the fastener and washer assembly. Use pliers to tighten set screw.
  • Raise the door back in place by inserting the hook of the Attic-Ease Reach Hook into the Fashion Pull-Ring.
  • Locate a convenient place to store the Attic-Ease Reach Hook. A hall closet is usually ideal. Place the Reach Hook in the stowing position and mark where the storage hook should go.
  • Hand turn the hook into position. Hang up your Attic-Ease Reach Hook.
